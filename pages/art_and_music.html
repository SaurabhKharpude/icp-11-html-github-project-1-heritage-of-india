Classical Dance Forms Like:

1. Bharatanatyam


Location :Tamil Nadu
Information :

  • Bharatanatyam is a classical dance form originating from the Indian state of Tamil Nadu.
  • It is one of the eight major classical dance forms in India and is known for its elegance, precision, and storytelling.


    • History of Bharatanatyam :
  • Bharatanatyam has its roots in the ancient Hindu temples of Tamil Nadu, where it was performed by Devadasis (temple dancers) as a form of worship and storytelling.
  • The dance form was initially known as "Sadir" or "Dasi Attam" and was later renamed Bharatanatyam in the 20th century.


    • 2. Odissi


    Location :Odisha
    Information :

  • Odissi is a classical dance form from the Indian state of Odisha (formerly known as Orissa).
  • It is one of the eight major classical dance forms in India and is known for its elegance, precision, and expressiveness.

    History of Odissi :
  • Odissi has its roots in the ancient temples of Odisha, where it was performed by Maharis (temple dancers) as a form of worship and storytelling.
  • The dance form was initially known as "Odramagadhi" and was later renamed Odissi in the 20th century.

    1. Hindustani Music:


    Location :North India
    Information :

    1. Hindustani Music:

  • Originated in North India, Hindustani music is known for its complex ragas and talas.
  • It is characterized by the use of instruments like the sitar, tabla, and tanpura.

    • 1. Sitar: A stringed instrument used in Hindustani music.
    2. Tabla: A percussion instrument used in Hindustani music.

  • Carnatic Music:


    • Location :South India
    Information :

    2. Carnatic Music:

  • Originated in South India, Carnatic music is known for its intricate melodic patterns and complex rhythms.
  • It is characterized by the use of instruments like the veena, mridangam, and kanjira.

    • 1. Veena: A stringed instrument used in Carnatic music.
    2. Mridangam: A percussion instrument used in Carnatic music.
