Afreen Ali, a character in the 2022 movie Sita Ramam, is originally from Pakistan. She is a student at a university in London and the president of the Pakistani student body. In the movie, Afreen is tasked with delivering a letter to Sita Mahalakshmi in India. The letter was written by Lieutenant Ram and is 20 years old. Afreen agrees to deliver the letter to get her share of her grandfather's property.

Afreen is a key link in the love story of Lieutenant Ram and Sita Mahalakshmi. When Afreen is asked to apologize to the Indian, she refuses and is given one month to pay compensation. Afreen returns to Pakistan to get the money from her grandfather, who gives her a letter to deliver to Ram. Afreen sets out to find Ram and uncovers the story of Sita and Ram

Afreen is a rebellious Pakistani student in London who sets fire to an Indian's car in retaliation for India burning the Pakistani flag.
