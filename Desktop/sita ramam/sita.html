Sita Mahalakshmi is later revealed to be princess Noor Jahan. Sita Mahalakshmi is a woman from The History of Ayodhya who is known for her beauty, strength, loyalty, courage, and determination. She believed that love and loyalty could not be replaced.

In the 2022 film Sita Ramam, Sita Mahalakshmi is a dance teacher who lives in Hyderabad's Noor Jahan's palace. The film is about an orphan soldier named Lieutenant Ram who receives a letter from Sita and sets out to find her.
