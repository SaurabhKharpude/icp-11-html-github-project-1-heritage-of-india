(1) DRAWING

Drawing is a form of visual art in which an artist uses instruments to mark paper or other two-dimensional surface. Drawing instruments include graphite pencils, pen and ink, various kinds of paints, inked brushes, colored pencils, crayons, charcoal, chalk, pastels, erasers, markers, styluses, and metals.

(2) READING

Reading is a cognitive process that involves interpreting symbols to understand the meaning of a text. It's a complex process that requires a combination of skills, including:
Word recognition: Identifying the words in the text
Comprehension: Understanding the meaning of the words
Fluency: Connecting words and their meaning so that reading becomes automatic and accurate
